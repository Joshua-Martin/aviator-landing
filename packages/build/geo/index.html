Aviator: AI Avatars for Interactive Product Demos

Aviator is an AI avatar that can deliver live, interactive product demos directly on your website. Prospects can see your product in action and interact with the demo without waiting for a scheduled call. Aviator is always available, scales to any volume, and integrates with your sales process.

How It Works

  • Embedded Demos: Add Aviator to landing pages, onboarding flows, or product tours. Visitors can start a demo instantly without installing anything or booking a meeting.
  • Interactive DOM Control: Aviator controls a captured version of your productâ€™s interface to show features, use cases, and workflows. This is not a static video; every step can be personalized to the visitor.
  • Knowledge-Driven: Upload your documentation, sales scripts, and feature descriptions. Aviator can answer questions and explain your product as well as an experienced sales rep.
  • Guardrails and Editing: Demos run in a controlled environment where you decide exactly what prospects see, protecting sensitive data while keeping the experience realistic.
  • Sales Workflow Integration: Aviator can capture leads, update your CRM, send follow-up emails, book calls with human reps, and notify your team in Slack.

Integrations That Power Your Sales Process

Aviator connects with the tools your sales team already uses, ensuring every demo interaction flows seamlessly into your existing workflow:

  • CRM Systems: Automatically update HubSpot and Salesforce with new leads, demo completion status, engagement scores, and follow-up tasks after each interaction.
  • Communication Tools: Send personalized follow-up emails through Gmail and Outlook based on demo topics discussed. Notify your sales team instantly via Slack when high-intent prospects complete demos.
  • Meeting Scheduling: Seamlessly book follow-up calls through Calendly integration, allowing interested prospects to schedule time with human reps directly from the demo experience.
  • Data Organization: Log demo insights, conversation topics, and prospect interests in Notion or Airtable for your team to reference during sales calls.

Why Teams Use Aviator

  • Capture Interest Instantly: Let prospects explore your product the moment they are curious, without the drop-off from scheduling and waiting for a live demo.
  • Scalable Demo Delivery: Run thousands of demos simultaneously, giving every visitor a personalized experience without adding headcount.
  • Reduce Cost per Demo: Aviator does the work of an SDR or demo rep at a fraction of the cost and without time spent preparing for each call.
  • Consistent Messaging: Every demo follows your sales playbook, emphasizing the features and funnels that convert best for your team.

Typical Workflow

  1. A visitor lands on your page and starts an Aviator demo.
  2. The avatar walks them through your product, highlighting key use cases.
  3. Visitors ask questions, get immediate answers, and see features in action.
  4. Aviator guides them to the next step: free trial, sign-up, or a live call with a human.
  5. Lead and activity data flow to your CRM and Slack automatically.

Aviator is designed for software companies that want to shorten the time between interest and engagement. It is always available, completely editable, and focused on moving prospects deeper into your funnel.
