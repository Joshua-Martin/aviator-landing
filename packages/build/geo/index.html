Aviator: AI Avatars for Interactive Product Demos

Demos can be embedded into landing pages, product tours, or onboarding flows. Visitors can watch the avatar guide them through real product functionality—no code required.

Capabilities

  • Interactive DOM Control: Avatar has the ability to control the demo via DOM actions, showing features and specific use cases that are actually useful to clients. These are not static demos.
  • Higher Conversion Rates: No call scheduled to call attended drop off—Aviator is always ready to do a demo, 24/7.
  • Poised to Sell: Aviator does more than show features. It knows your best selling strategies and funnels, and encourages users into the sales funnel that fits their persona—whether encouraging a free trial or booking a call to talk to a human about pricing.
  • Full Product Knowledge: With access to customer-specified knowledge base—upload your docs, sales scripts, and feature descriptions—Aviator becomes as knowledgeable as your most seasoned sales rep.
  • Built-in Guardrails: Demos are done with a captured Mock app that you can edit after the fact to ensure Aviator shows prospects exactly what you want them to see (and not what you don't).
  • Stack Integration: Aviator updates your CRM, sends follow-up emails, books calls with humans as needed, and can even message you on Slack under conditions you set.

Business Outcomes

More Pipeline

Get inbound prospects into the pipeline when they are most interested, capturing opportunities at the peak of engagement.

No More Dropoff

Eliminate the booked-to-attended drop-off rate with instant demos available whenever prospects are ready.

Infinitely Scalable

Run thousands of demos per hour, reaching more prospects than humanly possible.
